Bidens


Latin name: Bidens Alba

Other common names: Spanish needles, beggarticks, demon spike grass, needle grass

Family: Asteraceae (Compositae)

Parts used: usually arial parts (leaves); potancy of leaves greater than roots.


Main Actions

  • Antibacterial, anti-inflammatory, antimalarial, antimicrobial, antiseptic, astringent, immunomodulant, mucus membrane tonic.
  • Used to treat any systemic infections accompanies by mucus membrane problems anywhere in the body (chronic diarrhea, dysentery, UTIs, vaginitis, inflammed respiratory passages); systemic staph; malaria, babesia, leishmania; any other organisms Binens are active agains (Pg. 130).
  • Also used as a pot herb in many cultures
